# Global Summary - Audio Examples 🎙️

## Quick Reference - What You'll Hear

---

## 🌅 Morning (5 AM - 12 PM)

### Light Activity
> **"Good morning. Here's your notification. Alice messaged you on WhatsApp."**

### Moderate Activity
> **"Good morning. Here's what you missed. Sarah sent you 3 messages on WhatsApp. John called you. Email from Amazon."**

### Heavy Activity
> **"Good morning. You've been busy! Here's your summary. Alice sent you 8 messages, and Bob sent you 5 messages on WhatsApp. Sarah, Mike, and John called you. 7 emails, including 2 important. Several YouTube notifications."**

---

## ☀️ Afternoon (12 PM - 5 PM)

### Light Activity
> **"Good afternoon. Here's your notification. John called you."**

### Moderate Activity
> **"Good afternoon. You've got quite a few updates. Mom sent you 5 messages on WhatsApp. Dad and Brother called you. 3 emails from Netflix."**

### Heavy Activity
> **"Good afternoon. You've been busy! Here's your summary. 15 WhatsApp messages from 5 contacts. 8 missed calls from 6 people. 12 emails, including 3 important. Instagram activity from your friends. Several YouTube notifications."**

---

## 🌆 Evening (5 PM - 9 PM)

### Light Activity
> **"Good evening. Here's what you missed. Alice texted you. Email from Boss."**

### Moderate Activity
> **"Good evening. You've got quite a few updates. Sarah and Mike messaged you on WhatsApp. Lisa called you twice. Emails from Amazon and Google."**

### Heavy Activity
> **"Good evening. You've been busy! Here's your summary. Sarah sent you 10 messages, and Mike, Lisa, and John messaged you on WhatsApp. 5 missed calls from 4 people. Important email from Boss. Instagram activity from Alice and Bob. Several YouTube notifications."**

---

## 🌙 Night (9 PM - 5 AM)

**No greeting, direct summary:**

> **"Here's what you missed. Mom sent you 3 messages on WhatsApp. One missed call from Dad. Email from Amazon."**

## 🎯 Real Scenarios

### Scenario: Busy Workday
**Notifications:**
- WhatsApp: 12 messages (Alice: 5, Bob: 4, Charlie: 3)
- Calls: 3 missed (John: 2, Sarah: 1)
- Gmail: 7 emails (2 important)
- YouTube: 5 videos

**You Hear:**
> **"Good afternoon. You've been busy! Here's your summary. Alice sent you 5 messages, and Bob sent you 4 messages on WhatsApp. John called you twice. Sarah called you. 7 emails, including 2 important. Several YouTube notifications."**

---

### Scenario: Quiet Evening
**Notifications:**
- WhatsApp: 1 message from Mom
- Email: 1 from Amazon
- YouTube: 1 notification

**You Hear:**
> **"Good evening. Here's what you missed. Mom messaged you on WhatsApp. Email from Amazon. One YouTube notification."**

---

### Scenario: Social Day
**Notifications:**
- WhatsApp: 8 messages (4 people)
- Instagram: 10 notifications
- Calls: 2 missed (friends)

**You Hear:**
> **"Good afternoon. You've got quite a few updates. 8 WhatsApp messages from 4 contacts. Alice and Bob called you. Instagram activity from your friends."**

## ⚡ Key Features

✅ **Natural Greetings** - Good morning/afternoon/evening  
✅ **Smart Intro** - Based on notification count
