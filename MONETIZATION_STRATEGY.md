# Notiva AI - Monetization & Marketing Strategy

## Current Status: Free App (Version 1.0)

### Why Start Free?
- Build user base and gather feedback
- Establish reputation and trust
- Generate reviews and ratings
- Identify most valuable features
- Debug and improve stability

## Monetization Options

### Option 1: Freemium Model (Recommended)

**Free Version Features:**
- Basic notification capture
- View notifications by app
- Simple summaries (limited to 10 per day)
- Standard TTS
- 7-day notification retention
- 2 voice options

**Premium Version ($4.99/month or $39.99/year):**
- Unlimited AI summaries
- Advanced AI models
- Extended retention (up to 365 days)
- 10+ premium voices
- Priority support
- Notification backup/restore
- Cross-device sync (future)
- Custom notification rules
- Export to PDF/Email
- No limitations

**Implementation:**
- Use in-app purchases via Google Play Billing
- Offer 7-day free trial
- Implement gracefully (no ads, just feature locks)

### Option 2: One-Time Purchase
**Price:** $9.99 - $14.99
- All features unlocked forever
- No recurring charges
- Simpler for users
- Lower long-term revenue

### Option 3: Tiered Pricing

**Basic (Free):**
- 5 AI summaries per day
- 7-day retention
- Standard voice

**Pro ($2.99/month):**
- 50 AI summaries per day
- 30-day retention
- 5 voices
- Priority detection

**Ultra ($4.99/month):**
- Unlimited everything
- 365-day retention
- All voices
- Advanced features
- Early access to new features

### Option 4: B2B/Enterprise
**Target:** Companies for employee productivity
**Price:** $50-100/user/year
**Features:**
- Company-wide deployment
- Admin dashboard
- Analytics and reports
- Custom branding
- Dedicated support
- Integration with company tools

## Monetization Timeline

### Phase 1: Months 1-3 (Launch & Growth)
- Free for all users

- **Goal:** 5-10% conversion rate

### Phase 3: Months 7-12 (Optimization)
- Refine pricing based on data
- A/B test different price points
- Add more premium features
- Consider enterprise offerings
- **Goal:** 15% conversion rate

### Phase 4: Year 2+ (Scale & Expand)
- Consider white-label licensing
- Explore partnership opportunities
- International expansion
- Platform expansion (iOS)
