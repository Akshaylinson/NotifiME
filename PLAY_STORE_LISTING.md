## Full Description (4000 characters max)

📱 **Notiva AI - Your Intelligent Notification Assistant**

Take control of your notifications with Notiva AI, the privacy-first Android app that captures, organizes, and intelligently summarizes your notifications using advanced AI technology.

🌟 **KEY FEATURES**

✨ **AI-Powered Summaries**
• Get instant summaries of your notifications using the Gemma AI model
• Generate daily, weekly, or custom digests
• Summarize by app or across all notifications
• Save time by understanding what's important at a glance

🔊 **Text-to-Speech Integration**
• Listen to your notifications hands-free
• Perfect for driving, cooking, or multitasking
• Customizable voice, speed, and pitch settings
• Bluetooth audio support

📊 **Smart Organization**
• Notifications grouped by app
• Daily notification counter that resets at midnight
• Priority detection (High/Medium/Low)
• Search and filter capabilities

🔒 **Privacy-First Design**
• 100% offline - no cloud, no internet required
• All AI processing happens on your device
• Automatic masking of sensitive data (OTP, card numbers, emails)
• No telemetry or tracking
• Your data stays on your device

🎯 **Intelligent Priority Detection**
• Automatically identifies important notifications
• OTP and verification codes marked as high priority
• Banking and transaction alerts highlighted
• Social media and promotional content filtered

⚙️ **Customizable Settings**
• Adjustable speech rate and pitch
• Notification retention period (7-90 days)
• Auto-read new notifications
• Multiple voice options
• Dark mode support

💾 **Local Storage**
• SQLite database for fast access
• Automatic cleanup of old notifications
• Export capabilities (coming soon)

🎨 **Beautiful Material Design 3 UI**
• Modern, intuitive interface
• Smooth animations and transitions
• Consistent design language
• Easy navigation

📱 **Perfect For:**
• Busy professionals managing multiple apps
• People with visual impairments
• Anyone wanting to reduce notification overload
• Users who value privacy and offline functionality
• Multitaskers who need hands-free notification access

🚀 **How It Works:**
1. Grant notification access permission
2. Notiva AI starts capturing your notifications
3. View organized notifications by app
4. Get AI summaries or listen via TTS
5. Manage with smart priority filtering

⚡ **Technical Highlights:**
• Built with Flutter for smooth performance
• Powered by Gemma AI model
• SQLite for efficient data management
• Android NotificationListenerService integration
• Optimized for minimal battery usage

🔐 **Permissions Required:**
• Notification Access - To capture and display your notifications
• Post Notifications - To show app notifications (Android 13+)
• Internet - For initial AI model setup (one-time only)

🎯 **Use Cases:**
• "Summarize my WhatsApp notifications from today"
• "Read my important notifications aloud"

• "Give me a daily digest of everything"
